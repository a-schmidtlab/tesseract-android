Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes the privacy practices for TesseractAx ("the App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information Collection

Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal information directly. The only data collection that occurs is what Apple automatically collects through the App Store and iOS system, which may include:

  • Basic device information (model, iOS version)
  • App Store interactions
  • App crash reports (if any)
  • App analytics (if you have enabled this in your iOS settings)

This information is collected and managed by Apple according to their privacy policy. For more information about Apple's data collection practices, please visit: Apple's Privacy Policy

Third-Party Access

We do not share any user data with third parties because we do not collect any data directly.

Children's Privacy

Our App complies with Apple's App Store policies regarding children's privacy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.
